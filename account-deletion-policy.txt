App: Laos Travel
Developer: STLSMV Tech Co., Ltd.

To delete your account:
1. Open the Laos Travel app
2. Go to Profile > Account Settings
3. Tap “Delete Account” and confirm

Deleted Data:
- Personal info (name, email, phone)
- Booking history
- Saved favorites

Retained:
- Legal/compliance-related records (e.g., invoices) for up to 6 months
